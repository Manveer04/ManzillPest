Thermal Fogging

This type of fog produces fine chemical droplets, which will allow the droplets to be suspended in mid air to kill flying mosquitoes, flies and other insects. It is effective in control mosquitoes at large outdoor areas, such as parks, farms, and golf courses, where conventional sprays may not be effective. It is also used in indoor areas like warehouses, factories, and commercial buildings, where there is a need for fast and efficient control of flying insects.
