Common Flies

Insect flies trap light spectrum such as the Ultra Violet (UV) light. Flies that are attracted to it will adhere to the sticky sheet located inside the light trap.The sticky sheet contains a special glue that keeps the flies trapped and prevents them from flying away. Suggested To All Food & Beverages Outlet's to prevent food contamination and spread of diseases
