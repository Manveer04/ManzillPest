About Our Company

Welcome to Manzill Pest Control, your trusted solution for pest control services. We have been serving the Kuala Lumpur and Selangor area for 6 years, providing top-notch pest extermination and prevention services to residential and commercial properties.

Our team of highly trained and certified technicians use only the most effective and eco-friendly methods to get rid of pests and keep your property safe. From termites to bed bugs, ants to rodents, we have the expertise and experience to handle all types of pest infestations.

At Manzill Pest Control, we believe in taking a proactive approach to pest control. That’s why we offer a range of ongoing pest management programs to prevent infestations from happening in the first place. Our services are designed to be as convenient and hassle-free as possible, so you can focus on the things that matter most.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to provide the best possible service at an affordable price. We are proud to have earned a reputation as one of the most reliable and trustworthy pest control companies in the area. If you’re in need of effective and affordable pest control services, look no further than Manzill Pest Control. Contact us today to schedule an appointment and say goodbye to pests for good.
