Baiting

Bait in general, is a mixture of fodod above Ground Bait Station, it will be installed at an active termite (Cellulose) and insecticide for the infestation area where the bait will be consumed by the workers and they will subsequently bring the poison food back to the colony (Nest) to share with the other members. This process is known as the "colony elimination" process, and it is highly effective in controlling termite infestations. The advantage of baiting over traditional soil treatment methods is that it is less invasive and does not require drilling or trenching around the foundation of a building. Additionally, baiting can be used to target specific areas of a structure where termites are most active.
